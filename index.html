About the Brokerage

Built by an Active Operator

Conrad Cruz Real Estate Services is a Utah-based brokerage founded by Matt Strong, principal broker and active real estate operator. Matt has personally executed over 425 fix-and-flip transactions and has funded more than $380 million across 1,300+ real estate loans through Sierra West Funding, Conrad Cruz's sister lending company. He owns and manages a 25-property rental portfolio across the Salt Lake area.

That operator background shapes how Conrad Cruz works with clients. Whether you're selling a home, buying an investment property, or running the numbers on a flip, you're working with people who do this themselves — not just talk about it. We're a no-nonsense brokerage built for clients who think in spreadsheets and value substance over polish.

Our roster includes 12 licensed agents serving buyers, sellers, and investors across the Wasatch Front.
